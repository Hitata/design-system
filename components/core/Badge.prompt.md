**Badge** — a small mono uppercase status/label chip; use sparingly and let tone carry meaning (moss = success, brass = premium).

```jsx
<Badge tone="success" dot>Active</Badge>
<Badge tone="primary" solid>Hỏa</Badge>
<Badge tone="neutral">Draft</Badge>
```

Tones: `neutral` (cement), `primary` (ember), `success` (moss), `premium` (brass), `ink` (ink-slate). `solid` fills; `dot` adds a leading status dot. Square corners, hairline border.
